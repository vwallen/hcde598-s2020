Boom Vise

Each side of the boom vise is comprised of three nested parts. A threaded mount is attached to the boom mount. An inner sleeve fits into the threaded mount. A threaded cap, fits over the sleeve and screws onto the mount. When the cap is tightened, the sleeve compresses the boom, holding it in place.

Camera Mount

Use a plate socket similar to the quickshoe socket on the tripod. Rather than a turnable locking screw, use a sliding plate held in place by two bolts and springs. The top would have a thumbplate to allow the locking wedge to be slid back for changing out the camera.
