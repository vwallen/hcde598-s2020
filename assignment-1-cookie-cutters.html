Assignment 1: Cookie Cutters

When I first moved to Seattle, I knew nothing about opera.

In 2012, Seattle Opera performed a production of Puccini's Turandot, which my wife and I attended on a whim. Much to my surprise, I loved it. The next performances we attended were the complete Wagner Ring Cycle (15 hours of opera in one week!). This was a lot, or as a friend put it, "You don't just run a 5K and decide to sign up for a marathon!"

Since then, we have become regular attendees of Seattle Opera, which sadly has cancelled the remainder of the season due to the pandemic. I decided to channel my disappointment into baked goods, and have chosen to create cookie portaits of well-known composers: Giuseppe Verdi, Giacomo Puccini, and Richard Wagner.

Process

  1. Initial image treatment

    Photoshop: increase contrast, remove background, add perimeter outline, crop

  2. Illustration

    Clip Studio Paint: draw over source image

  3. Convert to curves

    Adobe Illustrator: outline illustration, initial shape cleanup to simplify curves

  4. 3D modeling

    Rhinoceros: extrude surfaces, create perimeter cutting surface

  5. Prepare for printing

    Cura: slicing, printing profile, gcode
